Sudoku and Wordoku FAQ

How do you play Sudoku?

Fill every row, column and 3 by 3 box with the digits 1 through 9 exactly once. Use the given digits to determine the only valid value for each empty square.

What is Wordoku?

Wordoku uses nine distinct letters instead of the digits 1 through 9. The logic is identical to Sudoku: every letter appears once in each row, column and 3 by 3 box.

Is Ninefold free to play?

Yes. Ninefold is free to play in a web browser and does not require an account or sign-up.

Can I play Ninefold on an iPad or phone?

Yes. Ninefold has responsive layouts and touch-friendly controls for phones, iPad Mini, iPad Air and iPad Pro in portrait and landscape.

How many Ninefold puzzles can I play?

You can start as many new games as you like. Puzzles are generated with randomized layouts, so an exact repeat is possible but uncommon.
