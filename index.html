    Four ways to solve

    Play Sudoku, Wordoku, Word Search and Nonogram online

    Ninefold brings number logic, letter-based Wordoku, English or Hindi hidden-word searches and picture-forming Nonograms together. Choose any difficulty and return to your saved puzzle anytime.

    01

    Classic Sudoku

    Complete the 9 by 9 grid so every row, column and 3 by 3 box contains each digit from 1 to 9 exactly once. Each puzzle has a logical solution.

    02

    Letter-based Wordoku

    Wordoku follows the same rules using nine distinct letters. It offers the familiar Sudoku logic in a different visual form without changing the way the puzzle is solved.

    03

    Word Search

    Choose English or Hindi, then find themed words hidden horizontally, vertically or diagonally. Higher levels use larger boards and can place words in any direction.

    04

    Nonogram picture puzzles

    Read the number clues for each row and column, fill confirmed pixels and mark empty squares with an X. Every completed grid reveals a monochrome mosaic.

    Useful answers

    Puzzle game FAQ

    How do you play Sudoku?

    Fill every row, column and 3 by 3 box with the digits 1 through 9 exactly once. Use the given digits to determine the only valid value for each empty square.

    What is Wordoku?

    Wordoku uses nine distinct letters instead of the digits 1 through 9. The logic is identical to Sudoku: every letter appears once in each row, column and 3 by 3 box.

    How do you play Word Search?

    Find each listed word hidden horizontally, vertically or diagonally in the letter grid. Words may also appear backwards on higher difficulty levels.

    Can I play Word Search in Hindi?

    Yes. Open Word Search and choose Hindi from the language menu to play themed Devanagari puzzles at any difficulty level.

    How do you play a Nonogram?

    Use the number clues beside each row and column to identify consecutive groups of filled squares. Mark confirmed empty squares with an X until the hidden pixel picture is revealed.

    Is Ninefold free to play?

    Yes. Ninefold is free to play in a web browser and does not require an account or sign-up.

    Can I play Ninefold on an iPad or phone?

    Yes. Ninefold has responsive layouts and touch-friendly controls for phones, iPad Mini, iPad Air and iPad Pro in portrait and landscape.

    How many Ninefold puzzles can I play?

    You can start as many new games as you like. Puzzles are generated with randomized layouts, so an exact repeat is possible but uncommon.
